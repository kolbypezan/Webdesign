Our Mission

Like flowers, FLORES (Fellow Latine Organizers Reconnecting and Educating in the Suburbs) nurtures the growth of Latine leadership and community in suburban areas. We blossom through shared knowledge via popular education, enrich our soil through community service, and honor our roots with a deep connection to Pachamama, cultivating a thriving network of engaged organizers.

Peciolos

Our work is organized into several key areas, our "peciolos," each focusing on a vital aspect of community empowerment.
